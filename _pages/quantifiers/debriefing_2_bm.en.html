Possibility of a smile manipulation

For some of the participants in the experiment (randomly selected), the video they saw of their conversation partner was filtered to make the person appear either more smiling or less smiling than they actually were. These participants interacted with a real person who was unaware that we had enhanced or reduced the smiles on their face. This digital manipulation, for the participants for whom it occurred, is quite realistic. However, a certain number of participants had completely normal interactions without any faces being filtered.
