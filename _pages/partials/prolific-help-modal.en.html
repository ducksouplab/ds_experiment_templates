In case of problems during the experiment

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the Chrome browser and that you are connected to the internet
  • Wait a few seconds (more than 25) and reload the page. Do not close the page.
  • If the other person can't hear you try to disconnect your microphone and reload the page
  • Sometimes, the interaction won't start even after a few seconds. In that case, please wait until the interaction time is over (4 minutes). The experiment will continue automatically. Please don't quit the experiment. you will still be compensated for your participation if you finish
  • If you had problems to talk with several participants, please try to change you internet connection (e.g., switch to a 4G hotspot) and reload the page.
